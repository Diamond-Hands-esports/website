We raised from an emergency situation during the first quarter of 2021. In the urge of creating a new home and the Gamestop Wallstreetbets meme happening, we decided on the team name Diamond Hands. It is a big meme for sure but for us also an important message.

If you have something valuable to you, keep it and be loyal to it. No matter how bad the situation is looking. For us these are mainly our players and our friends and family around us who we want to “diamond hand”.

Memes rise and die swiftly and the diamond hands meme is already from yesterday. But the message will stay for us until eternity.

CHRISTOPHER KRAFT, CEO DIAMOND HANDS ESPORTS
